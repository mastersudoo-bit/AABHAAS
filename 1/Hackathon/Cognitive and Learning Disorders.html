🧠 Cognitive and Learning Disorders in Children

📉 Intellectual Disability

What it is: Below-average intellectual functioning and limitations in daily life skills.

Example: Trouble with problem-solving or understanding concepts at the same rate as peers.

Support: Special education, structured routines, and life skills training.

🔡 Dyslexia

What it is: A learning disorder that affects reading and language processing.

Example: Difficulty recognizing letters or connecting sounds to words.

Support: Multisensory reading programs and literacy-focused interventions.

⚡ ADHD (Attention Deficit Hyperactivity Disorder)

What it is: A neurodevelopmental disorder that includes inattention, hyperactivity, and impulsivity.

Example: Difficulty staying focused or sitting still in class.

Support: Behavior therapy, classroom accommodations, and sometimes medication.

🧩 Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD)

What it is: A developmental disorder affecting communication, behavior, and social interaction.

Example: Difficulty understanding social cues or repetitive behaviors.

Support: Speech therapy, structured environments, and individualized education plans (IEPs).
