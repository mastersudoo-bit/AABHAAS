💥 Behavioral and Emotional Disorders in Children

🚫 Oppositional Defiant Disorder (ODD)

What it is: A pattern of angry, irritable mood, argumentative behavior, and defiance.

Example: Frequently arguing with adults or refusing to comply with rules.

Support: Behavioral therapy, consistent discipline, and parent training.

⚖️ Conduct Disorder

What it is: A pattern of aggressive behavior that violates the rights of others or societal norms.

Example: Bullying, physical fights, or stealing.

Support: Therapy, positive reinforcement strategies, and possibly medication.

😰 Anxiety Disorders

What it is: Excessive fear or worry that interferes with daily activities.

Example: Avoiding social situations due to fear or worrying excessively about school performance.

Support: Cognitive-behavioral therapy (CBT), relaxation techniques, and sometimes medication.

😞 Depression

What it is: A persistent feeling of sadness, hopelessness, and loss of interest in activities.

Example: Withdrawing from friends, difficulty concentrating, or feeling worthless.

Support: Therapy, family support, and possibly medication.
